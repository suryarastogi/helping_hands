## v136 — Gitignore E2E/Playwright artifacts and commit message quality hardening

**Status:** Completed
**Created:** 2026-03-12

## Goal

Two self-contained improvements from TODO.md:

1. **Gitignore E2E/Playwright test artifacts** — `frontend/test-results/`, `frontend/playwright-report/`, and `frontend/blob-report/` directories are generated by Playwright but not gitignored, causing accidental commits of large test artifacts.
2. **Commit message quality hardening** — `_parse_commit_message()` accepts trivially short/meaningless messages like `feat: -` or `feat: ...`. Add minimum-length validation and reject messages that are just punctuation/ellipsis. Also harden `_commit_message_from_prompt()` to reject trivially short extracted text (single char, punctuation-only).
